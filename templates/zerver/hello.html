Zulip: Workplace chat, organized.

Zulip’s unique threading model organizes your chat messages into conversations. Lots of conversations at once, spread across many days? No problem. With Zulip, you read exactly what matters to you.

Zulip is available on major Mobile and Desktop Operating Systems.

Features

SEE ALL FEATURES

Organized

Tired of important discussions being disrupted by automated messages, lunch plans and random links? Follow each conversation separately with Zulip.  Want to find a conversation from two months ago?  Zulip's fast and powerful search experience will delight you.

Beautiful Messaging

Express yourself just how you like with markdown formatting, hundreds of emoji, code highlighting and full internationalization. Share content with drag-and-drop file uploads, link previews and inline images. 

Fully Featured

Zulip has everything you want in group chat. Apps for the iPhone, Android and desktop.  More than 60 integrations (GitHub, Jira, Stripe, Zendesk, etc.). Keyboard shortcuts. Emoji reactions.  Need anything else?  We probably have it.

Open Source

Want chat that’s customized for your needs? Zulip is 100% open source software, and has the largest active development community among open source group chat applications.  With 80,000 words of developer documentation, a high quality code base and a welcoming community of hundreds of developers from around the world, it’s easy to jump in.
