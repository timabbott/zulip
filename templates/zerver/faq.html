Frequently asked questions

Does the Zulip SaaS service provide free hosting for open source projects and other worthy causes?
Yes, the Zulip SaaS service is free for open source projects and various other worthy causes. Contact support with details on your organization to request a sponsorship. We also have a detailed guide on Zulip for open source projects.
If I start out on the Zulip SaaS service, and a few years down the line want to move to running my own server, can I later export my data?
Yes! Zulip supports exporting your organization's data and then importing it into another Zulip server.
How can I see what Zulip looks like in a busy organization?
You can join a public Zulip organization like the Zulip development community, browse the message history, and chat with the development community. That server has several thousand user accounts and hundreds of active users.
